🚀 How to Submit Your Notes
1

Fork the Repo

Click Fork the Repo at the top of this page to copy it to your own GitHub account.

2

Open the Session Folder

Go to classes/ and click the folder for the session you attended.

3

Download the Template

Click the template file, then click Raw, then right-click and choose Save As.

4

Rename the File

Replace yourname with your real name, e.g. microsoft-entra-oyimafu-emmanuel.md

5

Fill in Your Notes

Open in Notepad, VS Code, or any text editor and fill in what you learned.

6

Upload & Pull Request

Upload to the session folder on your fork, then open a Pull Request. We review before merging.
